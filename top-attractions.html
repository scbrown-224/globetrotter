Top Attractions

Dunns River Falls

A stunning, terraced waterfall near Ocho Rios where visitors can climb the natural steps and enjoy the refreshing cascades

Seven Mile Beach

Located in Negril, this picturesque stretch of white sand and turquoise waters is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying vibrant beachside activities

Blue Hole

A hidden gem in the hills of Ocho Rios, the Blue Hole offers crystal-clear waters, thrilling jumps, and lush surroundings for an unforgettable adventure activities
