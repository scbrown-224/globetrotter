Events!

Carnival

Various locations across Kingston, Montego Bay, and Ocho Rios

Jamaica Carnival is a vibrant, energetic celebration held every April, featuring colorful parades, lively music, and elaborate costumes. The streets come alive with soca, dancehall, and reggae rhythms as participants dance and revel in the festive atmosphere, making it a must-attend event for anyone seeking to experience Jamaica's cultural exuberance

Reggae Sumfest

Catherine Hall Entertainment Complex, Montego Bay, Jamaica

Held annually in July, Reggae Sumfest is the largest and most celebrated reggae music festival in the world. It showcases the best of Jamaican music, featuring performances from top reggae, dancehall, and international artists. With its electrifying performances and enthusiastic crowds, Reggae Sumfest offers an unforgettable experience for music lovers

Portland Jerk Festival

Katawud Village in Ginger House, Rio Grande Valley, Portland, Jamaica

Celebrated every July, the Portland Jerk Festival is a culinary delight that highlights Jamaica's famous jerk cuisine. Visitors can enjoy a wide variety of jerked meats, seafood, and other traditional Jamaican dishes while enjoying live music, cultural performances, and family-friendly activities. This festival is perfect for food enthusiasts and those looking to immerse themselves in Jamaican flavors and culture
